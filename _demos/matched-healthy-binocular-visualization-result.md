This demo documents a matched binocular visualization result used as a healthy reference condition. The visualization shows how paired left-eye and right-eye image streams can be combined to support a stable binocular percept.

The matched case is useful as a baseline for comparing later experimental conditions, including altered alignment, simulated ocular deviation, or strabismus-like visual experiences. Additional figures, quantitative results, and detailed interpretation can be added here as the experiment record grows.
